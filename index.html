About Us

Rooted in Kerala's Coconut Heritage

Tecsify Exportings was born from a deep passion for Kerala's greatest natural treasure — the coconut. Established in Tirur, Malappuram, we've spent over 15 years building relationships with the finest coconut farmers across the Malabar Coast.

Today, we're one of Kerala's trusted coconut export houses — sourcing directly from farmers, processing in our certified facility, and delivering to global markets with transparency, consistency, and care.

🎯 Our Mission

To connect Kerala's finest coconut produce to the world's markets, delivering unmatched quality and reliability with every shipment.

🌟 Our Vision

To be India's most trusted coconut export brand, synonymous with purity, sustainability, and global quality standards.

Quality Control Process

From grove to port, every step is monitored and documented.

01

Farm-Level Sourcing

Direct procurement from 500+ registered farmers. Each farm is periodically audited for pesticide usage and harvest practices.

02

Grading & Sorting

Mechanical and manual grading for size, weight, and defects. Only Grade A produce advances to processing and packaging.

03

Processing & Fumigation

Cold storage, drying, and fumigation as per destination country requirements including ISPM-15 treated wooden crates.

04

Lab Testing & Certification

Third-party lab tests for moisture, FFA, microbial, and pesticide residue. Certificates of Analysis provided with each shipment.

05

Packaging & Shipping

Custom or standard packaging, containerized and reefer shipping from Kochi, Nhava Sheva, or Mundra ports.
